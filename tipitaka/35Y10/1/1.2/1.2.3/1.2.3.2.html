1016 »  Na cakkhu na indriyanti?

1017 Cakkhuṃ ṭhapetvā avasesā indriyā na cakkhu, indriyā. Cakkhuñca indriye ca ṭhapetvā avasesā na ceva cakkhu na ca indriyā.
